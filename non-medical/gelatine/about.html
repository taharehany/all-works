About Gelatine:

Gelatin is extracted from collagen and is a high molecular weight protein composed of 18 amino acids. Bovine hide. Bones and fish skin are normally used for the production of gelatin.

Gelatin can adsorb 5 – 10 times of water, if heated it converts to solution state. Upon cooling, it enters jellying state.

Because of its special properties, gelatin is used in making jelly, pharmaceutical capsules, and industrial adhesives. In addition, gelatin is widely used in other applications.
