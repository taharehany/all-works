Payments

  • payment methods

    • Payment methods are available via cash, checks, and credit cards. New types of payment methods can also be added.

  • Credit / debit cards

    • All electronic payments are processed by external payment terminals.

  • Split the tenders

    • A single order can be paid as a split payment between multiple parties, as well as separate payment methods.

  • Round the coin

    • Prices and payments can be rounded to the smallest denomination of currency.

  • Offline payments

    • Offline requests are synced when reconnected.

  • Billing

    • Prepare and print invoices for your business customers.

  • Accounting

    • Payments are directly integrated into the Odoo Accounting system to make the registration process simple and reliable.

  • Customer advice

    • Supports customer gratuities, whether for an additional amount or by means of converting change into tip.

Customer loyalty

  • Customer record

    • Easily identify your customers by registering their email and contact address, allowing you to view discounts and keep track of individual sales.

  • Select the customer

    • You can look to your customers with the built-in search feature or learn about them using the barcode printed on their loyalty card.

  • Building block system

    • Record your customer's VAT number and include it in invoices.

  • Loyalty cards

    • Reward customers with loyalty points and exchange them for gifts or discounts. Points can be earned depending on the product, demand or sale value.

Restaurant management

  • Floor plans

    • Assign orders to tables and receive an overview of your restaurant's floors as well as make changes on the go with the graphical editor.

  • Seat management

    • Track your guests with a summary of your restaurant capacity and table availability.

  • Kitchen print

    • Send order instructions to bar and kitchen printers. Instructions can be sent to different printers automatically depending on the product category.

Web application

  • Browser support

    • Odoo Point of Sale System is a web based application that can be downloaded to any device and operating systems that run Chrome, Firefox or Safari.

    Microsoft Windows, Apple OSX, Linux, Android and iOS are all supported operating systems.

  • iPad first

    • The Odoo Point of Sale System is used on modern iPad or Android devices of similar size and performance.

  • Fixed computer compatibility

    • Odoo's POS system can also be used on stationary computers, tablets, and industrial touch screens.

  • Work without an internet connection

    • The Odoo Point of Sale system remains in operation even during network disconnection. The web browser can be disconnected from the network without losing data.

  • HTML5 styles

    • Odoo's POS system is open source and can be customized with HTML5 / JS expansion modules.
